Learn More

Creativity

Vincent aspires to be as creative as possible in his works, taking inspiration from the works of nature and from other artists.

Imagination

Imagination is necessary when creating images of places that Vincent might have never seen before. It is key to creating unique images.

Originality

Vincent attempts to be original in creating pieces even when referencing other art, to provide something new and fresh with each work.

About Vincent

Vincent is currently a sophomore at Drexel University studying Computer Science and has been creating pixel art since highschool, mostly as a hobby.

Interests:

  • Computer Games (Favorite Games: Hearthstone, osu!, League of Legends)
  • Music (Favorite Genres: EDM, House, Electronic)
  • Dance (Favorite Style: B-boying)
  • Programming (Favorite Languages: HTML, CSS, C++)
  • Pixel Art (Favorite Types: Landscapes, Repeatable Textures, Sprites)

Vincent is beginning his adventures in 2D animation, in hopes to be able to quickly churn out spritesheets.
